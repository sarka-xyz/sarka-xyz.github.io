Biography
Born and raised in Thessaloniki, Greece, Feymi Mustafa Oğlu’s creative journey began long before his formal education

Falling in love with graphic design at age 10, he spent his formative years blurring the lines between visual aesthetics and sonic experimentation

By age 15, adopting the alias “SARKA” (the Greek word for flesh) he began producing experimental house music, finding a raw, rhythmic language that would eventually become the heartbeat of his design philosophy

Feymi’s professional path was cemented early, graduating from a Vocational High School in Graphic Design
